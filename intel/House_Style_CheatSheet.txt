Xlights House Style Cheat Sheet
==============================

Core Groups to Target:
- Metronome_Outlines — roof, windows, doors; your “timekeeper”
- Arches_All / Arches_Odd / Arches_Even — motion lanes + call/response
- Focal_Tree — chorus engine; carries the hook
- Focal_VerticalMatrices — Front Door L/R; secondary focal for riffs/lyrics
- Focal_Spinners — PopStars/Showstopper; hit markers & cadence accents
- 01 – All Display / 02 – House (All) / 03 – Yard (All) — wideners (use sparingly)
- All_Display_NoFaces — house‑wide lifts without lighting faces
- House_Third_Left / Middle / Right — spatial phrasing (antiphonal patterns)

Effects Default Kit:
- SingleStrand → metronome/chases/bounce on outlines & arches
- Spirals → musical lift on Focal_Tree (thin in verses, wider in choruses)
- Shockwave → quick, bright hit accents (downbeats/transitions)
- On (short pops/fades) → punctuation; never long strobe
- Shader (subtle) → wide texture during holds; keep dim

Section Recipes:
- Intro: Metronome_Outlines = SingleStrand (dim, slow) • 1× Shockwave on a pickup • micro‑blackout (≤0.3s) before first hit
- Verse: Arches bounce on Beat • Focal_Tree minimal Spirals (low arms/speed) • V‑Matrices thin On pops on motifs
- Pre‑Chorus: Add width: All_Display_NoFaces low‑amp Shader • Shockwave on cadence
- Chorus: Focal_Tree Spirals (arms↑ rotation↑) • Arches bounce + occasional Shockwave on 1 • Outlines steady chase
- Bridge: Pull density down; monochrome; negative space + short Off cuts
- Finale: Stack short Shockwave + On hits across Focals → brief All‑Display lift → end on dark

Timing & Hits:
- Tracks: Beat/Downbeat + Bar + optional Vocals
- Hit pattern: tiny blackout just before big accents; Shockwave ≤ 0.4s; full white ≤ 0.8s total in a row

Brightness Caps:
- Wide groups: 65–80%
- Focals: 75–90% for peaks only
- Verses: 35–55% most of the time

Color Habits:
- Verses: 1–2 colors + warm‑white accents
- Choruses: add third color or widen saturation
- Finale: brief full palette, then cut

Do / Don’t:
- ✅ One clear focal at a time
- ✅ Use thirds (Left/Middle/Right) for call‑and‑response
- ❌ Don’t layer multiple fast textures across All_Display
- ❌ Don’t leave full‑white running; keep hits short
